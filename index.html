01 / about

Hi, I'm Sameer 🤖

I'm a robotics and AI enthusiast based in Pune, India, currently working in Team CiPHER. My focus is the full loop of building a mobile robot — modeling it in CAD, simulating it in Gazebo, and getting it to navigate on its own with ROS2.

Right now that means AMR Simulation: a differential-drive robot that can see with LiDAR and a camera, build a map of its world, and almost turn corners without an opinion about it. When I'm not in Gazebo, I'm usually breaking something in Python — like a terminal AI chatbot that talks back.
